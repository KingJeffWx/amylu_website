time | Sept - Oct 2019
 role | Web Design (Figma)
   Visual Design (Procreate, Illustrator)

  • Project Overview

As part of the course Programming Usable Interfaces, I designed and developed a cinnabon website using Figma, HTML, CSS, & JavaScript. The site is hosted on Github. The cinnabon prompt was assigned randomly, while each student had free reign over the design style and implementation choices. You can view the final website here: cinnabon.

  • Competitive Analysis

We began the process through a competitive analysis of three ecommerce websites that allowed for item purchasing. I focused on Laduree, COS, and La Colombe, and analyzed 2 strengths as well as 2 areas that could use improvement.
